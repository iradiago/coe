About the COE Departments

Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering

The Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering Program was first proposed to be offered in Botolan Campus under the Self-Liquidating Program Scheme in early 2002 under the leadership of President Feliciano S. Rosete.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering began in the 2nd Semester of AY 2001-2002, approved as a self-liquidating program under the Institute of Evening Opportunity Program (IEOP).

Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering

The Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering began when the RMPC Board of Regents approved its curriculum.

Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering

The Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering started with the RMPC Board of Regents approving its curriculum.
